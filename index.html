About Us

Spruce Up Wash is a Fresno-based pressure and soft washing company dedicated to helping homeowners and businesses restore curb appeal, protect property value, and maintain clean, healthy environments. Our mission is to provide reliable, professional, and eco-conscious exterior cleaning services with a customer-first approach.


We aim to establish Spruce Up Wash as a trusted local brand, leveraging competitive pricing, quality workmanship, and personal customer relationships to build repeat business and referrals.
